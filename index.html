About Me

I've always valued the role of logos in society. After graduating high school in technology, I realized it wasn't the right path for me. A short Graphic Design course at Lisbon School of Design led me to pursue a bachelor's in Global Design at IADE.
Here you can see some of my best projects.




What I Do

3D I can create anything my 16-inch laptop can handle, which is perfect if you enjoy watching paint dry!

WEB I create stunning websites that are user-friendly, except for that one user who insists on using Internet Explorer.

BRAND I look on the internet for visual references and then combine them to create my own original work.

DESIGN I can design just about anything; all it takes is some amazing ideas, time and a generous amount of coffee.
